Cookies 'n' Cream 3-ingredient Fudge

A simple and tasty bite-sized treat!

This is a nice and easy recipe to make.

It is a nice change of pace from more common desserts like Oreos and chocolate fudge.

Ingredients

  • White Chocolate
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Oreos

Steps

  1. Put the chocolate and sweetened condensed milk and melt it in the microwave in 15-second increments.
  2. When the chocolate is fully melted, add in the chopped Oreos and stir until mixed.
  3. Pour the mix into a parchment paper lined baking tin.
  4. Chill for 2 hours oro until firm.
  5. Cut into bite-size pieces.
