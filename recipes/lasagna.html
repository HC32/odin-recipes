World's Best Lasagna

A lasagna that someone already snuck a piece from!

A delicious traditional Italian pasta dish.

It is made of layered wide, flat pasta and fillings.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the sauce, noodles, and ricotta mixture.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
